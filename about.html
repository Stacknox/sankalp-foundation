Our Story

It began with a resolve.

In 2026, in the heart of Silchar, a small group of friends sat together with one uncomfortable question: if not us, then who?

They had no office, no funding and no grand plan — only a shared sankalp, a resolve, to do something real for the Barak Valley. That winter, they bought what blankets they could afford and walked them, street by street, to the families who needed them most.

That single drive became a movement. Today, Sankalp Foundation is a growing circle of volunteers who give their time, skills and hearts — proof that you don't need to be extraordinary to do extraordinary good.

Who We Are

We come from every walk of life

A lawyer, a schoolteacher, a police officer, a shop owner, an IT engineer — by day we lead very different lives. By heart, we share one purpose. That diversity is our greatest strength: every skill, every perspective, every connection becomes a way to help.

Lawyers Teachers Police Officers Business Owners Healthcare Workers Students Everyday Citizens IT Professionals

A lawyer stands up for families who have no one to speak for them. A teacher gives a child the future their circumstances tried to deny. A police officer becomes a protector far beyond the uniform. A business owner turns resources into relief that reaches the doorstep. A healthcare worker brings care to those who could never go looking for it. And an IT professional carries our work further than our footsteps ever could. Different hands — one resolve.

What Drives Us

Mission, vision & values

Our Mission

To uplift the underserved of the Barak Valley through direct, dignified action — warmth, relief, education and care — powered entirely by community volunteers.

Our Vision

A Silchar where no one is left behind — where compassion is ordinary, and every person has the dignity, support and opportunity to build a better tomorrow.

Our Values

Dignity first. Total transparency. People over paperwork. And the simple conviction that small acts, done with heart, add up to real change.
