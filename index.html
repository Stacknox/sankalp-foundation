Our Mission

Small acts, done with heart, change lives.

Born in Silchar in 2026, Sankalp — meaning resolve — was founded on a simple belief: that a community grows stronger when it cares for its weakest. We channel the kindness of ordinary people into real, on-the-ground help, so no family in the Barak Valley ever has to face hardship alone.

What We Do

Four ways we serve our community

Every programme is designed around dignity — meeting people where they are, with what they truly need.

Winter Warmth

Blanket and warm-clothing drives that reach families through the cold Barak Valley winters — door to door, no one left shivering.

Crisis Relief

Rapid response when disaster strikes — from the COVID-19 pandemic to seasonal floods — delivering food, essentials and care to the vulnerable.

Education & Dignity

Supporting underprivileged children with school supplies, books and encouragement, so that circumstance never decides a child's future.

Community Care

Health awareness camps, support for the elderly, and small acts of everyday kindness that keep our neighbourhoods whole.
